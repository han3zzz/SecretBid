Documentation
1
Commit Phase
Submit keccak256(amount, nonce, address) on-chain. Your actual bid stays completely hidden until the reveal window opens. A deposit locks your position — forfeited if you don't reveal.
On-Chain Sealed Deposit
2
Reveal Phase
After bidding closes, submit your plaintext amount + nonce. The contract verifies your hash matches. Invalid or missing reveal forfeits your deposit. Highest valid bid wins the auction.
Verify Hash Highest Wins Nonce Key
3
REX Privacy
Route through the REX relay to mask your identity at the transaction level. Zero-knowledge proofs verify bid validity without ever exposing your on-chain address to other bidders.
ZK Proof REX Relay Anonymous
4
Settlement
Winner pays the winning amount and receives the item. All losing bidders get their full deposits refunded automatically. Any excess deposit over the winning bid is also returned instantly.
Auto Refund Winner Claims On-Chain
